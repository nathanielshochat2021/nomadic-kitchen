# Operations & Deposit Terms

> Operational playbook. Replace timelines, percentages and terms with your real
> policy and run them past counsel before publishing as terms of sale.

## Build flow

1. **Quote request** — customer configures a build on the site and submits the
   quote form. The exact spec + estimated total arrives by email (Resend) and is
   logged server-side.
2. **Confirm & quote** — we reply within [1–2 business days] with confirmed
   pricing, lead time and any spec clarifications.
3. **Deposit & reserve** — a **50% deposit** reserves the build slot and locks
   the configuration. Materials are ordered.
4. **Build** — the cart is built one at a time, by hand. Progress photos shared
   at [milestones].
5. **Balance & handoff** — remaining **50% balance** due on completion, before
   delivery or pickup.

## Deposit terms (placeholder)

- **Deposit:** 50% of total, due to reserve a slot.
- **Balance:** 50%, due on completion prior to release.
- **Configuration lock:** spec is locked at deposit; changes may affect price
  and lead time.
- **Cancellation:** deposit is [partially refundable before materials are
  ordered / non-refundable after]. Define clearly.
- **Lead time:** typically [X–Y weeks] from cleared deposit; confirmed per order.

## Delivery

- **Local pickup** always available.
- **Delivery** quoted per location (customer provides city/state in the quote
  form). For trailer builds, road-legal handoff; for caster builds, crated or
  local delivery.

## Materials & supply

- Track lead times for: stainless cooktops, fridge drawers, trailers, solar +
  battery components, wood stock.
- Maintain a preferred-supplier list with backups for long-lead items.
- Buy long-lead items at deposit, not before, to avoid inventory risk.

## Quality & warranty (define)

- Finish/seal inspection before handoff.
- Document a workmanship warranty period and what's covered (wood, joinery,
  hardware) vs. third-party components (cooktop, fridge — pass through mfr.
  warranty).

## Capacity planning

- Track shop hours per build by configuration tier.
- Cap concurrent active builds to protect lead times and quality.
- Use the quote pipeline to forecast material orders.
