# Go-To-Market

> Channel and messaging plan. Replace targets and budgets with your numbers.

## Objective

Generate qualified quote requests for built-to-order Nomadic Kitchen carts, at a
cost per qualified lead that supports healthy margins.

## Target audiences

1. **Patio entertainers** (primary revenue) — homeowners, 35–65, value design
   and craft. Channels: Instagram/Pinterest, home & garden, local.
2. **Overlanders / adventurers** — 4x4, vanlife, camping communities. Channels:
   YouTube, Instagram, overlanding forums & events.
3. **Tailgaters / hosts** — gift-able, statement piece. Channels: seasonal
   social, sports communities.

## Core message

**"The kitchen that follows you outside."** Hand-built, wood-clad, made to order
— from the patio to the trailhead. Premium, editorial, photography-led.

## Channels

| Channel | Role | First moves |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Instagram** | Hero channel — craft + lifestyle | Build-process reels, finished-cart photography, configurator teasers |
| **Pinterest** | Discovery for patio/design intent | Pin gallery shots → landing page |
| **YouTube** | Depth for overlanders | "Build a kitchen that tows anywhere" film |
| **Email** | Nurture quote requests | Auto-reply + sequence to deposit |
| **Local / events** | High-intent, in-person | Farmers markets, overlanding meets, home shows |
| **Referral** | Post-delivery | Photo + referral ask at handoff |

## Funnel

1. **Attract** — social + search → landing page.
2. **Engage** — configurator (the hook): let people build their dream cart.
3. **Capture** — quote form carries the exact spec (low-friction, high-intent).
4. **Convert** — fast personal reply → deposit.
5. **Delight** — progress photos, great handoff → referrals & UGC.

## Metrics

- Sessions → configurator starts (engagement rate)
- Configurator starts → quote submissions (capture rate)
- Quotes → deposits (close rate)
- CAC, AOV, attach rate, lead time satisfaction

## Budget (placeholder)

Start lean: organic social + a small paid test on Instagram/Pinterest retargeting
configurator visitors. Reinvest from closed deposits.
