# Business Model

## What it is

Nomadic Kitchen is a **hand-built, wood-clad outdoor kitchen cart**, sold
**built-to-order**. It is not a camper and not a food truck — it's a premium
outdoor cooking station for homeowners, entertainers and overlanders.

## What we sell

- **Base cart** — a complete, usable outdoor kitchen at a fixed starting price
  ($9,950 placeholder). Solid-wood cabinet, stainless 3-burner cooktop, prep
  counter + sink, lower shelf, locking casters, matte seal.
- **Paid add-ons** — refrigeration, power, water, towing, weather protection,
  comfort and personalization. Each add-on carries its own price (see
  `lib/configurator.ts`, the single source of truth).

Average order value is the base price plus a meaningful attach rate on add-ons;
the configurator is designed to make upgrades feel natural, not pushy.

## Who it's for

1. **Patio entertainers** — want a beautiful, permanent-feeling outdoor kitchen
   without a built-in masonry project. Roll-anywhere on casters.
2. **Overlanders / adventurers** — want a trailer-mounted, off-grid kitchen
   (solar, water, fridge drawers) that tows behind their vehicle.
3. **Tailgaters / event hosts** — want a road-legal, eye-catching cooking rig.

## How we make money

- **Made-to-order, deposit-backed.** No inventory risk. A 50% deposit funds
  materials; the balance is collected on completion.
- **Margin on base + attach.** The base price covers materials, shop time and a
  target margin; add-ons carry their own margin.
- **Low overhead.** One-at-a-time builds, direct-to-consumer via the website.

- **Genuinely hand-built in solid wood** — not a wrap, not flat-pack.
- **One product, infinitely configurable** — simple to communicate, easy to
  upsell.
- **Editorial brand** — premium positioning supports premium pricing.
